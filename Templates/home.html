About Us


Who doesn’t want to be healthy? We live in a world where everyone is racing against time and pace. People are suffering from a lot of illnesses due to the changing environment and stressful life. Women are having early menopause and infertility due to late marriages and lifestyles. With growing pollution and exposure to new viruses, people suffer from various respiratory illnesses. We want a doctor who can be compassionate, and at the same time, be approachable as your friend.
Welcome to VR CHEST AND WOMEN CARE CLINIC. The clinic was conceived with the ideology of providing the state of the art facilities in the management of respiratory and women’s health by Dr. Vasunethra Kasaragod and Dr. Veni Nekkilady.

Dr. Vasunethra Kasaragod
Dr. Vasunethra Kasargod is a well-known pulmonologist in Bangalore whose main specialties include asthma, allergy, and interventional pulmonology. He has completed his Med-school at the reputed Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute. Later, the doctor obtained his Specialty MD degree in pulmonology (lung diseases) from Lokamanya Tilak Medical college and hospital, Mumbai, which is one of the best institutes for the same in India. He later on obtained the prestigious European Diploma in Respiratory Diseases from the European Respiratory Society in France. He has gained rich experience in Pulmonology being associated with various Institutes in Bangalore for 11 years. He treated thousands of patients suffering from lung problems, mastered the skills, and is currently working as a Consultant Interventional Pulmonologist at the premier Manipal Hospital Miller’s Road, Bangalore.
Dr. Veni N
Dr. Veni Nekkilady is a gynecologist whose main area of interest includes laparoscopy and high-risk pregnancy. She graduated from the prestigious Bangalore medical college and went on to be a rank holder in AIIMS Delhi from where she attained her specialty in gynecology. At AIIMS, she was guided by the best gynecologists in India and treated various patients suffering from infertility, high-risk pregnancy, and cancer, catering to patients from all over India. Her passion was to learn the art of healing. She went on to advanced training in laparoscopy surgery from Kochi, Kerala. She has worked as an Assistant professor at St. Johns medical college and MS Ramiah Medical College, Bangalore for 5 years. She has been associated with Cloud Nine and Motherhood Hospitals for 4 years as a Consultant Gynecologist.
